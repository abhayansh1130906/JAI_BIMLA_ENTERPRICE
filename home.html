About Us

Founded by Sanket Trivedi and Saksham Trivedi, JAI BIMLA ENTERPRISES PVT. LTD. honors the legacy of our beloved grandparents who inspired us to live with values and vision. We are a registered merchant exporter under the GSTIN: 09AAGCJ6551Q1ZS, based in Meerut, Uttar Pradesh.

We are proudly associated with the Export Promotion Council of Uttar Pradesh and operate under the legal frameworks required by the Indian Government.

Buyer Onboarding Requirements

To ensure a smooth transaction, we require the following documents from our buyers:

  • Copy of Importer's Certificate (IC)
  • Copy of PAN Card (or equivalent national ID)
  • Original Bank AD Code Letter
  • Bank-Attested IFSC/SWIFT Code Letter
  • Request Letter (for freight & forwarding instructions)
  • Buyer's Company Registration Certificate
  • Embassy verification for Documentation (if required)
